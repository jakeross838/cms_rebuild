## Critical Gaps Requiring Roadmap Updates

### GAP 1: Safety & Compliance Management
**Pain Points**: #246-250, #581-590, #931-950 (40+ items)
**Priority**: P1 (Legal/liability implications)

**Missing Features**:
- Site-specific safety plans
- Toolbox talk documentation
- OSHA compliance tracking per trade
- Safety observation documentation
- Worker safety certifications
- Incident/near-miss reporting and tracking
- PPE distribution and compliance
- Emergency contact management
- Excavation safety documentation
- Crane/lift equipment certifications
- SDS (Safety Data Sheet) management
- Fire extinguisher/first aid inspections

**Recommendation**: Add to Phase 3 or create new Phase 3.5

---

### GAP 2: Environmental Compliance
**Pain Points**: #941-950
**Priority**: P1 (Regulatory requirements)

**Missing Features**:
- SWPPP (Stormwater Pollution Prevention Plan) compliance
- Turbidity barrier/silt fence inspections
- Tree protection documentation
- Noise/dust control compliance
- Environmental permit tracking
- Hazardous waste disposal documentation
- Wetland buffer zone compliance
- Construction debris recycling
- Protected species observation
- Air quality monitoring

**Recommendation**: Add to Post-MVP Phase 12 (Advanced Features)

---

### GAP 3: Lender Portal
**Pain Points**: #641-655 (15 items)
**Priority**: P2 (Nice to have for MVP, important for growth)

**Missing Features**:
- Real-time construction progress verification
- Draw request vs completion comparison
- Inspection reports tied to draws
- Lien waiver verification for funding
- Project budget and CO history access
- Draw history and loan balance tracking
- Projected vs actual draw timeline
- Insurance compliance verification
- Schedule and completion date visibility
- Document access for underwriting
- Early warning signs of project distress
- Contractor license/insurance verification
- Appraisal and market value tracking
- Cost benchmarking against market rates
- Builder performance across loans

**Recommendation**: Create new Post-MVP Phase (Lender Portal)

---

### GAP 4: HR / Time & Attendance ✅ ADDRESSED
**Pain Points**: #421-425, #357
**Priority**: P2 (Affects cost allocation accuracy)

**Missing Features**:
- Employee hours by project for cost allocation
- Employee onboarding paperwork management
- Vehicle mileage and expense reports tied to projects
- Workers' comp classification tracking
- Labor burden rates for cost estimating
- Time and attendance integrated with project costing

**Resolution**: Created **Phase 13.5: HR & Time Management**

---

### GAP 5: Equipment Management ✅ ADDRESSED
**Pain Points**: #187, #359, #600, #896
**Priority**: P2

**Missing Features**:
- Track equipment and tool usage on site
- Company vehicle and equipment assignments
- Equipment scheduling across projects
- Tool and equipment location tracking across sites

**Resolution**: Detailed in **Phase 13: Advanced Features**

---

### GAP 6: Data Portability & Migration
**Pain Points**: #1221-1230 (10 items)
**Priority**: P1 (Critical for customer acquisition)

**Missing Features**:
- Export all data in standard formats
- Import from Buildertrend, CoConstruct, Procore
- Data ownership guarantees
- API access for custom reporting
- Google Workspace/Microsoft 365 integration
- Data migration service for onboarding
- Parallel system operation during transition
- Historical project accessibility after migration
- Custom data fields and cost code structures
- Version history and audit trail on all data

**Recommendation**: Add Data Import/Export to Phase 1 or create dedicated migration tooling

---

### GAP 7: Realtor/Sales Agent Features ✅ ADDRESSED
**Pain Points**: #656-670 (10 items not covered in Post-MVP)
**Priority**: P3

**Missing Features**:
- Available lots and specifications
- Prospect preliminary budgets
- Builder capacity and estimated start dates
- Past project showcase
- Design configurator for prospects
- Prospect engagement tracking
- Standard features and upgrade pricing
- Commission agreement management
- Pipeline visibility to avoid competing referrals
- Builder offerings comparison

**Resolution**: Added to **Phase 12: Sales & Pre-Construction**

---

### GAP 8: Advanced Scalability Features
**Pain Points**: #1231-1240
**Priority**: P2 (Critical for target scale)

**Missing Features**:
- Multi-office/region management (partially in Phase 12)
- White-label client portal with custom branding
- Custom workflows without developer help
- Multiple entities/companies (partially in Phase 12)
- Industry benchmarking against anonymized data
- Franchise/license processes to other builders
- Multi-language support
- Joint venture/partnership management

**Recommendation**: Add white-labeling and benchmarking to MVP scope

# Module 36: Lead Pipeline & CRM

**Phase:** 5 - Full Platform
**Status:** TODO

## Description
Sales pipeline management from initial lead capture through proposal and contract. Tracks prospects, manages follow-ups, and converts won deals into active projects.

## Key Features
- Lead capture from web forms and manual entry
- Pipeline stages with drag-and-drop kanban board
- Lead scoring and qualification criteria
- Follow-up task scheduling and reminders
- Proposal generation from estimates
- Win/loss tracking and analysis
- Lead source attribution and ROI
- Conversion to project on deal close

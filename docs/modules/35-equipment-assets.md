# Module 35: Equipment & Asset Management

**Phase:** 5 - Full Platform
**Status:** TODO

## Description
Tracks company equipment, tools, and assets including location, maintenance schedules, and assignment to projects or employees. Reduces loss and ensures equipment readiness.

## Key Features
- Equipment/asset registry with details
- Assignment tracking (project, employee, location)
- Maintenance schedule and service history
- GPS/location tracking integration
- Check-out/check-in workflow
- Depreciation tracking
- Equipment cost allocation to projects
- Loss and damage reporting

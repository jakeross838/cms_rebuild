## Description
Tracks change orders from request through approval and budget impact. Manages the financial and scope changes that occur during construction with full audit trails.

- Multi-level approval workflow
- Cost impact calculation (materials, labor, markup)
- Schedule impact assessment
- Client-facing change order presentation
- Digital signature for approval
- Budget auto-adjustment on approval
- Change order log and reporting

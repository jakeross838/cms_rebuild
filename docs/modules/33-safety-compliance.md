## Description
Safety management including toolbox talks, incident reporting, safety checklists, and OSHA compliance tracking. Helps builders maintain safe job sites and proper documentation.

## Key Features
- Safety checklist templates (daily, weekly)
- Toolbox talk scheduling and attendance tracking
- Incident/accident reporting with forms
- Near-miss reporting
- OSHA compliance documentation
- Safety training record management
- Safety score tracking per project/crew
- Corrective action tracking

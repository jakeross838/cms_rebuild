# Module 31: Warranty & Home Care

**Phase:** 5 - Full Platform
**Status:** TODO

## Description
Post-construction warranty management and home care tracking. Enables builders to manage warranty claims, schedule service visits, and maintain client relationships after project completion.

## Key Features
- Warranty item registration per project
- Warranty claim submission (by client or builder)
- Service request workflow and scheduling
- Warranty period tracking with expiration alerts
- Vendor warranty pass-through management
- Home maintenance schedule and reminders
- Service history log per project/unit
- Warranty cost tracking and reporting

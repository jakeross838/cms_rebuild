# Module 30: Vendor Portal

**Phase:** 5 - Full Platform
**Status:** TODO

## Description
Self-service portal for subcontractors and suppliers to submit bids, upload invoices, manage lien waivers, view payment status, and update their company information.

## Key Features
- Vendor self-registration and profile management
- Bid submission interface
- Invoice upload and status tracking
- Lien waiver digital signature
- Payment history and status view
- Insurance certificate upload
- Schedule visibility for assigned tasks
- Communication with builder team

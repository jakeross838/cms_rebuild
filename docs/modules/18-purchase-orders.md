# Module 18: Purchase Order Management

**Phase:** 3 - Financial Power
**Status:** TODO

## Description
Creation, approval, and tracking of purchase orders to vendors and suppliers. Links POs to budget lines and enables three-way matching with invoices and receipts.

## Key Features
- PO creation with line items and cost codes
- Approval workflow based on amount thresholds
- PO status tracking (Draft, Sent, Acknowledged, Received, Closed)
- Three-way matching (PO, receipt, invoice)
- Partial receipt tracking
- Vendor PO delivery via email/portal
- PO templates for recurring orders
- Budget commitment tracking from POs

# Module 28: Punch List & Quality Checklists

**Phase:** 4 - Intelligence

## Description
Digital punch list management and quality inspection checklists. Enables systematic quality control throughout construction and streamlined closeout punch list resolution.

## Key Features
- Punch list item creation with photos and location
- Assignment to responsible parties
- Status tracking (Open, In Progress, Completed, Verified)
- Quality inspection checklist templates
- Room/area-based organization
- Priority and severity classification
- Batch status updates
- Completion reporting and sign-off

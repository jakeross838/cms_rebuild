# Module 38: Contract & E-Signature

**Phase:** 5 - Full Platform
**Status:** TODO

## Description
Contract template management, generation, and electronic signature collection. Covers construction contracts, subcontractor agreements, change orders, and other legal documents.

## Key Features
- Contract template library with variable fields
- Contract generation from templates with auto-fill
- Electronic signature integration (DocuSign, native)
- Multi-party signature routing
- Contract status tracking and reminders
- Amendment and addendum management
- Contract term extraction and tracking
- Executed contract storage and retrieval
